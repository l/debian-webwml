Security Information

Please send security-related bug reports to security@debian.org

Date reported: 13 May, 1998

Affected packages: shadow-su

Brief description:
We have received reports telling us that there was a problem with the program su from the shadow package. This has been fixed in recent uploads. We recommend you update shadow-su immediately if you have it installed on your system.

Vulnerable: Yes
